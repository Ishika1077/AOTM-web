Viksit India 2047 Overview

"Viksit Bharat @2047" is the vision to transform India into a developed nation by its 100th year of independence in 2047. The vision encompasses various aspecvts of development, including economic growth, social progress, environmental sustainability, and governance, It's collective aspiration of 14 billion Indians to see their nation amongst the most developed in the world.



Core Objectives

☑️ Economic Growth: Achieves sustained and inclusive econmic growth to ensure prosperity for all citizens and establish India as a global economic powerhouse.

☑️ Social Progress: Enhance human development by improving education, healthcare, and skill development to empower every individua and create a just and equitable society.

☑️ Environmental Sustainability: Build world-class,sustainable infrustructure to support activities and improves the quality of life while protecting our natural heritage.

☑️ Good Governance: Promote tranceparency, ifficiency, and citizens participants ib decision-making processes to ensure accountability and responsiveness.



Long-Term Goals

By 2047, India aspires to become a developed nation with a high-income economy, a robust and inclusive social system, and a global leadership role. this vision is not merely about economic metrics but about achieving a holistic transformation that touches every aspect of citizens life.


The theoretical underpinnings of this vision draw from modern development economics, emphasizing human capital development as a primary driver og growth. This involving massive investments in education, healthcare, and skilling to create a workforce that can compete globally. The goal is to move from a factor-drivers economy to an innovation-driven one, fostering a culture of research and development. enterpreneurship, and technological adoption.


Furthermore, the vision incorporates principles od sustainable development, recognizing that longg-term progress cannot come at the cost of environmental degradation. This means transitioning to a green economy, promoting renewable energy, and implementing policies that ensure ecological balance. socially, the goal is to eliminate poverty, reduce inequality, and ensure that the benefits of growth are shared by all section of society, creating a truly inclusive and equitable nation.
